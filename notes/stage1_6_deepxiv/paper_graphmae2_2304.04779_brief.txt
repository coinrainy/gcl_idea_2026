  "tldr": "[research paper] GraphMAE2 introduces two regularization strategies—multi-view random re-mask decoding and latent representation prediction—to improve feature reconstruction in masked self-supervised graph learning. Multi-view re-mask decoding injects randomness into the decoding process by repeatedly masking and reconstructing encoded embeddings, reducing overfitting to noisy or low-discriminative input features. Latent representation prediction enforces reconstruction in the embedding space using graph-derived representations as targets, enhancing robustness and generalization. The method achieves at least 2.45% improvement over state-of-the-art on ogbn-Papers100M (111M nodes, 1.6B edges), demonstrating superior performance under feature disturbance and low discriminability.",
